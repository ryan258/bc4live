It might surprise folks that I had anything but love in my heart when I wrote this song.

I was actually losing at love at the time. I had actually started a mini catalogue of rants about my many misfortunes and deadly encounters that was my love life.

I realized that my purgatory was quickly becoming hell and I was creating it.

Just like that I turned it around.

I wanted to feel like I was in love, the real love. Not the romanticized crap we see on tv but rather that love that words alone couldn’t express. That love which was more than a feeling but rather a knowing.

That’s the love I desire, that’s the love I want to be in.

It was my own fantasy that I was sending out to the universe, a sincere request. My heart was empty but I still had my imagination.

What would it be like to love somebody absolutely and have them love you back?....

Absolutely!

I used some Goombay rhythms with some old school soul, which could easily be a duet with the right female voice.

The passion must be visible, anything else just would not do.

I think recording this piece had more of an emotional impact on me compared to when I wrote it.

This song was already riddled with so much of my spirit that once I began to hear the music really come alive in the studio, something moved in me.

It was like a loving mother giving birth, such a heavy vibration that filled the room that affected everyone, including the engineers.

I knew we created something that may be bigger than ourselves. That is the point isn’t it?

To be involved in something greater than our selves.

When you love somebody speaks to that essence that all humans can relate to and is made from.

We are all made from and of love.
