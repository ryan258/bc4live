(Bahamian Scat)

Have you ever been betrayed?

People you swore were on the same page only to later realize that they were aiding in your destruction?

Well let me tell you. It sucks!!

After my band staged a mutiny, I was totally thrown off. I never even saw it coming...

One rehearsal we were laughing and enjoying ourselves, then the next week I found myself being verbally attacked whilst being insulted for being too ambitious.

It was one of my lowest moments of my life. I just didn’t understand. Everything was going well. What did they want from me?

Why were they trying to take my joy, my peace, my LIFE?

It took me a while to realize that they were just afraid and lacked the confidence even though we were pretty good. We were just not on the same page at all.

But when life gives you lemons, make lemonade.

I was already experimenting with Bush fusion at the time so I turned my frustration into a new project

It’s ironic that this song has been the toughest to record, but has been a crowd favorite with my new band called The Wibesmen.

Making this EP has been a life changing experience, I made the decision to take a leap of faith to live on purpose.

Never was afraid of failure because life could only get better. Right?

So I will believe that my dreams are reality and my best days are ahead.

I am on a journey that has already been laid for me but I just have to walk it.

There are many obstacles on the journey but you must be determined enough to push through.

There is always a way!
That’s the wibe right there,
the wibe of life!
