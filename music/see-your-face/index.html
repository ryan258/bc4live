Every once in a while life throws us a cold bucket of water. A sobering reality check which you are never prepared for.

The realization that something is out of our control can be even more debilitating.

At least it was for me.

I have always been fond of women and for the most part, they have always been very fond of me. Eventually my fondness got the better of me and what I thought was easily in control became a whirl wind of pure madness.

A beautiful baby boy was the best fruit our relationship ever produced. Life now had a new flavor and it was so sweet, you just knew nothing would ever be the same again.

But it wouldn’t last. I sank into the deepest undiscovered layer of the earth’s core only to see my child snatched away from my grasp whisked away to a foreign country.

This could never work!

Somethings are out of my control I admit, however, faith as small as a mustard seed can move mountains. I’ll choose faith, I’ll be patient and abide my time

“Till I see your face again.”
